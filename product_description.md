L'Ocean Sun Protection Formula
"Lotion From The Ocean" (LFTO)
Natural Ocean-Inspired Daily Moisturizer
Product Overview
This is a lightweight, natural oil-in-water (O/W) emulsion lotion designed for daily hydration and skin barrier support. It features ocean-derived botanicals (seaweed/kelp extracts), flaxseed gel, hyaluronic acid, and gentle plant oils. The formula targets a final pH of 4.0–4.5 for optimal skin compatibility and active ingredient stability. It is emulsified with Montanov systems for a stable, non-greasy feel.
Key Benefits (science-based):

Deep hydration from sodium hyaluronate and flaxseed gel.
Soothing and antioxidant support from aloe, tocotrienols, and red algae/sea kelp extracts.
Lightweight emollience from squalane and MCT oil.
Natural preservation system.
Mild fragrance for a fresh, ocean-inspired scent.

Target Market

Eco-conscious consumers aged 25–55 who prefer clean, natural skincare.
Beach/coastal lifestyle users and ocean lovers (branding emphasizes “From The Ocean”).
People seeking gentle, non-irritating daily moisturizers for face and body.
Those with normal-to-dry or sensitive skin who want hyaluronic acid + marine actives without heavy silicones or synthetic fragrances.
Potential positioning: after-sun care, daily hydration, or “ocean wellness” skincare line.
